Chapter 1: The First Encounter
Hana Yamamoto

The story unfolds at a prestigious college known for its blend of tradition and modernity, set on a sprawling campus that combines classic architecture with contemporary design.

Hana Yamamoto, the new transfer student, comes from a luxurious Japanese mansion in the city’s upscale district. Her home is a grand estate surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens, featuring classic architectural elements such as intricate woodwork and a serene inner courtyard with a koi pond, mirroring her family’s wealth and influence. Before transferring, Hana attended an elite school in Japan known for its rigorous academics and refined atmosphere, reflecting her family’s high social status.
