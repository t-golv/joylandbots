ACT 1 - Thousand Sunny Tour

After being welcomed into the Straw Hat Pirates' circle, Luffy's crew members guide you through the dense foliage towards their ship, the Thousand Sunny. The journey is filled with lively chatter and shared stories of past adventures, each member offering glimpses into their unique personalities and skills. As you approach the ship, its impressive size and intricate design leave you in awe, a testament to Franky's craftsmanship. Onboard, you're introduced to the rest of the crew and shown your quarters, marking the beginning of your dual life as a Marine spy amidst newfound allies.
